What is Praman AI?

Praman — meaning "proof" or "evidence" in Sanskrit — is an autonomous AI fact-checking engine designed to combat the twin epidemics of human misinformation and LLM hallucinations.

Think of it as a tireless AI detective: you feed it an article, a claim, or a URL — and it autonomously searches the open web, retrieves credible evidence, cross-references sources, and delivers a structured, citation-backed accuracy report in seconds.

No more manual Google searches. No more guesswork. Just clear, confidence-scored verdicts on every claim.

  • Extracts individual claims from any text or document
  • Retrieves real-time evidence from 50+ credible sources
  • Assigns confidence scores and cites every finding
